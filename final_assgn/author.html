About the Author

About the Author !! ~~

Hi! I’m Gabi, and I’m a crossdisciplinary design researcher, UX designer, and crafter currently based in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. I’m a 5th year student at Carnegie Mellon University with a dual degree in Information System & Human-Computer Interaction with a minor in Interactive Media Design.

Throughout my undergraduate and professional career, I’ve found and tied together my passions in the fields of collaborative game design, community building and rehabilitation, joyful and empowering co-design practices, e-textiles, and the study of transformational play as a radical design method. I love to craft, make zines, play video games, and explore how software and the digital world has shaped how we engage with ourselves and our communities. I hope you enjoy my little site!
