About the Author

Welcome to August’s world of Riso!

Hi! My name is August & I am an amateur maker/crafter & design researcher @ CMU! This is an interactive minisite about Risograph printing -- a printing technique & art form that began in Japan in the late 1950s. I love Riso printing because of what it enables me, and other makers in the Riso community to do, and I wanted to share what I love so much about it!

Riso is deeply community oriented and is impeccably close to my own heart. I hope you enjoy this little site and come to love Riso, printing, and the world of simple artmaking as much as I do : o]
